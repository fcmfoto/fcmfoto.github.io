portfolio artist's statement

Exquisite Corpses, DMA, 2019

Was a Meme team supreme Group project, in which we look at an end of the world scenerio, where cyborgs and pharmacutical industry takes over the world.

Glitch art, DMA, 2019

Was a Meme team supreme Group project, in which we look at an end of the world scenerio, where cyborgs and pharmacutical industry takes over the world. In this version we Glitch the image to add onto the feeling of distruction.

Emoji, DMA, 2019

When asked what emoji i would like to be in the phone, i said Pan dulce. Which I believe has become a piece of mexican culture known and accepted internationaly. It can also be used for various ways: for example, literally as bread or figuratively as money.

Composite Art, Photo collage, 2019

A project where I looked at the advancement of technology and the development sprawl amongst communities. The composite piece contained a photograph of El Captain composed of high rises, and the exploitation of elements to “advance” human civilization.

Deep sound, Audio, 2019

El Elotero, Video, 2019

El Elotero is a visual art piece ment to analyze the presence of central american vendor amongst the student body.

The Madonna of the Lilies [painting by William-Adolphe Bouguereau], Digital Interactive Art, 2019

This work was created as direct response to the recent events at United States southern border. Events the unfortunatley involved the seperation of children from there parents. when analyzing this issue i realized that there is a large number of supporters using cristianity to justify there doing. thus, by using christian paintings and iconography I create commentary as to was being done, as well as questioning the meaning of the religion itself.
